# Defensive Publication Notice

## Purpose

This repository and its associated documentation serve as a **defensive publication** to establish prior art for the **Unified GS1 Resolver Mesh** infrastructure and its implementation patterns.

**This document is intended to serve as a defensive publication and prior art disclosure under applicable patent laws, including 35 U.S.C. §102, and may be relied upon by patent examiners and standards bodies when assessing novelty and prior art.**

## What This Establishes as Prior Art

The following resolver infrastructure approaches, architectural patterns, and methods are disclosed:

1. **Multi-chain router architecture** for GS1 identifier resolution
2. **Deterministic contract deployment** at universal addresses across multiple blockchain networks
3. **Token-free, open-access resolution** of GS1 identifiers to product information
4. **Integration patterns** between blockchain-anchored resolution and open data sources (e.g., Open Food Facts)
5. **Neutral resolver mesh infrastructure** operating independently of governance token systems

**Note:** This specification describes **infrastructure for product identifier resolution**. It does not cover optimization methods, intelligence layers, or commercial services that may be built on top of resolver infrastructure.

## Relationship to Other Intellectual Property

The resolver mesh infrastructure described here is maintained as **open public infrastructure**. 

Separately, certain commercial optimization methods for agentic commerce networks (including methods for intelligent coordination, hyperdimensional computing, and reservoir computing approaches) are subject to patent applications filed by related entities. Those patent applications cover **commercial optimization layers that may utilize resolver infrastructure**, not the resolver infrastructure itself.

**The resolver infrastructure remains open and unencumbered** regardless of the status of separate patent applications covering commercial optimization methods.
